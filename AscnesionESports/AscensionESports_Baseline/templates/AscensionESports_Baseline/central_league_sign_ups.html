About the Leagues

Ascension Esports offers Leagues for all ranks.
All leagues run for 9 weeks, playing 2 Bo1 games each week in a single night.
All leagues start at 6 pm CST and each have their own day!

Please sign up for the League corresponding to your Solo-Queue rank for this past season and Pre-Season.
Only Elder League allow sign ups as a 5-man premade. We are allowing duos to sign up this season!

  • Roster release: 1/29
  • 1st Day of Dragon League: 2/12
  • 1st Day of Elder League: 2/14
