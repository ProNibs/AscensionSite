About the Leagues

Ascension Esports offers Leagues for all ranks.
All leagues run for 9 weeks, playing 2 Bo1 games each night.
All leagues start at 6 pm CST and each have their own day!

Please sign up for the League corresponding to your Solo-Queue rank for this current season.
Only Elder and Baron League allow sign ups as a 5-man premade. We do not allow duos to sign up this season.
