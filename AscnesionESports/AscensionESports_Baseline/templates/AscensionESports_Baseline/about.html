Prosperity Through Education, Elevation Through Competition, Success Through Community


Ascension Esports is a League of Legends oriented gaming organization with an emphasis on education, competition, and community. Founded by HouseParty5v5 League of Legends staff in 2016, Ascension delivers a rich experience and opportunity to connect and play with new friends.

Ascension operates three leagues each with a different rank bracket--bronze/silver, gold/platinum, and diamond plus--to promote competitive integrity. Your performance is important to us as they are important to you. We compile stats for all three leagues and display them for your convenience.

"We wanted to create an environment where education and competition can coincide to provide everyone with an opportunity for success." - Wynden, Ascension Esports Founder
